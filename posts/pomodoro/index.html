What is Pomodoro?

The Pomodoro technique was invented in the early 1990s by the developer and author Francesco Cirillo.The method is named “Pomodoro” after the tomato-shaped timer Cirilo used to track his work as a university student.1

The Pomodoro Technique Core Process2

  1. Choose a taks you’d like to get done.
  2. Set the Pomodoro for 25 minutes.
  3. Work on the task until the Pomodoro rings.
  4. When the Pomodoro rings, put a check mark on a paper.
  5. Every 4 pomodoros, take a longer break.
